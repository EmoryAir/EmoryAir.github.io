Air Pollution Map

Emory Air Quality is a student-led air quality sensor network. The first of its kind at Emory that displays real-time data around campus. This promotes awareness, general knowledge, and engagement for students and faculty alike about our interaction with the environment.

We are currently working on setting up sensors around campus, and are running sensors inside as models for the sensors outside.
